About Gabe

Programmer | Creator | Artist | Nature Lover | Rock Climbing Enthusiast

Gabe is a recently graduated computer science student and software developer with experience a variety of front-end and back-end technologies. He has built and published multiple Chrome extensions and websites, and is currently developing a Gmail-related web app. His work focuses on solving problems and taking on creative ideas no matter how much learning and research stands in his way.

In fact, Gabe is a manic creative in all areas of his life. Whether it is printing a 3D mouse prototype, constructing his own sit/stand desk, or even building his own home rock climbing wall, he has lots of experience bringing a vision to life. He can't wait to hear about where your ideas could take him!
