About Gabe

Programmer | Creator | Artist | Nature Lover | Rock Climbing Enthusiast

Gabe is a software developer with experience in both front-end and back-end technologies. He has built and published multiple Chrome extensions and is currently developing a Gmail-related web app. His work focuses on integrating APIs, automating workflows, and enhancing user experience through well-crafted solutions.

He emphasizes efficiency and innovation, leveraging AI to fill knowledge gaps and accelerate development. Gabe has experience with MongoDB, Oracle databases, and various web technologies, ensuring scalable and maintainable applications.

Beyond his personal projects, he is passionate about building practical, user-friendly tools and refining his expertise in modern web development. He is currently working on a portfolio website to showcase his art, his product 'GettaGripz,' and his education and experience.

In his free time, he enjoys designing in Fusion 360, working on creative projects, and staying up-to-date with the latest tech trends. He also has an interest in automation, digital product development, and optimizing workflows through AI and smart tooling.
